Our Story

When I first started working from home, I thought it would be a dream. No commute. No office distractions. Complete flexibility.

Instead, I found myself working 12-hour days, answering messages at 10pm, and feeling guilty every time I stepped away from my laptop. By the end of each week, I was completely burnt out.

I tried every productivity hack. Downloaded every app. Followed every "expert" on Twitter.

Nothing worked long-term. Because I was solving the wrong problem.

The issue wasn't that I needed better time management. It was that I needed a complete system for managing energy, setting boundaries, and preventing burnout.

After years of trial and error, I built that system. And it transformed everything.

Now I finish work by 4pm most days. I have clear boundaries. I actually enjoy the flexibility of remote work. And I want to help you do the same.

Our Mission

To help 10,000 remote workers build sustainable, fulfilling work-from-home careers without burnout, stress, or sacrificing their lives.
